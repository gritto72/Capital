Company Overview

Supporting the Petroleum Industry Since 1999

Capital Energy has long been committed to supporting the petroleum industry by addressing health and safety concerns across various operational aspects. We established our Air Loop & H2S Safety business unit to meet the industry's most stringent safety requirements.

Since inception in 1999, this unit has grown into one of Capital Energy's key areas of expertise — particularly across the UAE — providing high-quality products, comprehensive services, and after-sales support for onshore and offshore operations.
